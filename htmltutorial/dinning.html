POPULAR DISHES

>> Non-Vegetarian Dishes:

* Chettinad Chicken:
A spicy chicken curry made with a blend of freshly ground spices and coconut.
* Kozhi Varuval (Chicken Fry):
Spicy fried chicken with a crispy outer layer and a juicy interior.
* Mutton Chukka:
Tender pieces of mutton cooked with aromatic spices until dry and crispy.
*Chettinad Fish Curry:
A tangy and spicy fish curry made with tamarind and a variety of spices.
*Nandu Masala (Crab Masala):
A delicious crab curry made with traditional Chettinad spices.

> Vegetarian Dishes:

Paniyaram:
Savory dumplings made from fermented rice and urad dal batter, often served with chutney.
*Vazhaipoo Vadai:
Banana flower fritters that are crispy on the outside and soft inside.
*Kothu Parotta:
Parotta (flaky flatbread) chopped and mixed with vegetables, eggs, or meat, and cooked with spices.
*Vegetable Kurma:
A rich and creamy vegetable curry made with coconut and a blend of spices.

>>Rice-Based Dishes:

Biriyani:
A fragrant rice dish cooked with spices, meat (usually chicken or mutton), and a unique masala blend.
*Pongal:
A savory rice and lentil dish seasoned with black pepper, cumin, and ghee.
*Kavuni Arisi:
A sweet dish made from black rice, sugar, and coconut, often served during special occasions.

>>Snacks and Sweets:

*Murukku: A crunchy snack made from rice flour and urad dal flour, deep-fried to perfection.
*Athirasam:
A traditional sweet made from rice flour and jaggery, deep-fried to a golden brown.
*Seepu Seedai:
A sweet or savory snack shaped like a comb, made from rice flour.

>>Beverages:

Coffee:
Traditional South Indian coffee made with a metal filter, known for its strong flavor and aroma.
*Panakam:
A refreshing drink made with jaggery, water, and spices like cardamom and ginger tea.
